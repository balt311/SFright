Salesforce License Request 
announcement311 license account requests will now be made through the 311 Salesforce System

 Have your authorized agency representative submit by creating  a service request under
  ECC-311 CRM Account Request

The system admin team will review request & respond by email.
Once approved the licensee will need to attend Salesforce training prior to receiving access.

 You can request training by emailing  Salesforce311supportteam@baltimorecity.gov  
If you have any questions, feel free to call 311
